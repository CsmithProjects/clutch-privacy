Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2025

Clutch ("we," "our," or "the App") is operated by Conor Smith. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

The short version: We collect only what's needed to make the app work. We never sell your data. Your conversations are private. You can delete your account and data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type What Why
Account Info Email, full name, password To create and secure your account
Phone Number Stored as a one-way hash (SHA-256) — we cannot see your actual number To verify identity during signup and prevent duplicate accounts
Chat Messages Text you send to the Wingman AI, including screenshots you upload for analysis To provide AI-powered texting advice
Location Data Approximate location (only when using Date Spotter) To recommend nearby venues for dates
Subscription Info Plan type and status To manage your subscription and feature access

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the App's features — AI chat advice, date spot recommendations, bio writing, and panic button responses
  • Process your messages through AI — Your chat messages and uploaded screenshots are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate responses. See Section 4 for details.
  • Prevent abuse — Phone number hashes are used to prevent users from creating multiple free accounts
  • Manage subscriptions — Process payments and unlock premium features

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising or ad targeting
  • We do NOT share your conversations with other users
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do NOT store your actual phone number — only a one-way hash

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the App. Each has its own privacy policy:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Supabase Database, authentication Account info, chat history, subscription data
Anthropic (Claude AI) AI-powered responses Chat messages and screenshots (processed, not stored by Anthropic per their API terms)
Twilio SMS verification Phone number (only during signup verification)
RevenueCat Subscription management Purchase history, plan status
Google Places API Date Spotter venue data Approximate location (when using Date Spotter)

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure)
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption
  • Phone numbers are stored as irreversible SHA-256 hashes
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • We do not store your data on local servers — all data resides in Supabase's cloud infrastructure

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Chat history: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Phone hash: Retained as long as your account exists (to prevent re-registration abuse)
  • Rate limiting records: Automatically deleted after 1 hour

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data upon request
  • Opt out of any future communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at cmsmith708@gmail.com.

8. Age Requirement

Clutch is intended for users 16 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16. If you believe a user under 16 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
